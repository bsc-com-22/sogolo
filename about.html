Our Mission

Sogolo.com is revolutionizing Africa's social commerce landscape by providing a trusted verification platform that connects verified buyers and sellers across social media platforms. We are not a store ‚Äì we are the bridge that builds confidence in online transactions.

What We Do

Our platform verifies products and services across social media to make online trade safer and more reliable. We ensure that every transaction happens between authenticated parties, reducing fraud and building trust in Africa's growing digital economy.

Our Vision

To become Africa's most trusted verification platform, enabling seamless and secure transactions across all social media marketplaces while fostering economic growth and digital inclusion.

Why We Started

Africa's social commerce market is booming, but trust remains a significant barrier. We saw the need for a reliable third-party verification system that would protect both buyers and sellers, ensuring authentic products and transparent transactions.

Our Impact

Since our launch, we've helped thousands of sellers increase their sales by an average of 150% through verification. We've also protected countless buyers from fraudulent transactions, building a safer digital marketplace for everyone.
