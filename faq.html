Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers about how Sogolo.com helps you trade smarter and safer across social media.

What is Sogolo.com?

Sogolo.com is a trusted third-party verification platform that connects verified buyers and sellers across Malawi's social media marketplace. We verify products and services to make online trade safer and more reliable. Unlike marketplaces, we don't sell products ourselves—we verify the authenticity of items sold through social media platforms.

How does Sogolo.com make social media buying and selling safer?

We provide a rigorous verification process that authenticates sellers and their products. This includes verifying seller identity, product authenticity, and ensuring sellers meet quality standards. By displaying our verification badge, buyers can trust they're dealing with legitimate sellers. This reduces fraud, builds confidence, and creates a safer trading environment across social media platforms.

Does Sogolo.com sell products?

No, Sogolo.com does not sell products. We are a verification platform, not a marketplace or store. Our role is to verify and authenticate sellers and products that are sold across social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp. We provide the trust layer that makes these transactions safer for both buyers and sellers.

How does the verification process work?

Our verification process consists of three simple steps: 1) Sellers submit their products or services along with necessary documentation through our contact form, 2) Our verification team thoroughly inspects and verifies the authenticity, quality, and legitimacy of submissions, 3) Once approved, sellers receive our trusted verification badge to display on their social media profiles, giving buyers confidence in their authenticity.

Who are the "Tailors"?

"Tailors" are our team of verification specialists who carefully examine each product submission. They ensure that products meet our quality standards, verify seller credentials, and authenticate the legitimacy of items. Think of them as quality controllers who tailor the verification process to each category, ensuring thoroughness and reliability across all product types.

How do I make payments through Sogolo.com?

Sogolo.com facilitates secure payment processing between buyers and verified sellers. Payment methods vary by seller, but our platform ensures transparency and security throughout the transaction. We recommend using secure payment methods and always completing transactions through our verified channels to protect your funds. For specific payment questions, contact our support team.

What are the main product and service categories?

We verify products and services across all major categories including Fashion & Apparel, Electronics & Gadgets, Home & Living, Beauty & Cosmetics, Food & Beverages, Health & Wellness, Automotive, Real Estate, Services & Skills, and Handmade & Crafts. Our comprehensive verification covers virtually every item sold across social media platforms in Malawi.

How does Sogolo.com protect buyers from scams?

We protect buyers through multiple layers: 1) Rigorous seller verification ensures only legitimate sellers receive our badge, 2) Product authentication verifies the quality and authenticity of items, 3) Transparent seller credentials give buyers confidence, and 4) Safe transaction protocols protect payment information. Always look for our verification badge when making purchases from social media sellers.

How does Sogolo.com support sellers?

We support sellers by building their credibility and trust in the marketplace. Verified sellers typically see sales increase by an average of 150% because buyers trust our verification. We provide seller verification badges, help establish credibility, increase buyer confidence, and give access to a growing community of verified sellers. This professional verification elevates sellers above unverified competitors.

Is Sogolo.com available outside Malawi?

Currently, Sogolo.com focuses on Malawi's social media marketplace. Our goal is to expand our verification services across Africa, but for now, we serve buyers and sellers within Malawi. As we grow, we'll extend our trusted verification platform to other African countries, building trust across the continent's social commerce landscape.
