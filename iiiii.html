Your Questions, Answered.

Everything you need to know about Sogolo.com

What is Sogolo.com?

Sogolo.com is a trusted third-party verification platform that connects verified buyers and sellers across Malawi's social media marketplace. We verify products and services to make online trade safer and more reliable.

How does Sogolo.com work?

Our simple 3-step process: 1) Sellers submit their products or services for verification, 2) Our team inspects and verifies authenticity and quality, 3) Approved sellers receive our trusted verification badge to display on their social media.

Does Sogolo.com sell products?

No, we are not a store. We are a verification platform that connects verified buyers and sellers. We verify the authenticity of products and services sold across social media platforms, but we don't sell them ourselves.

How do I get verified?

Getting verified is easy! Simply contact us through our contact page, submit your products or services with necessary documentation, and our team will review your submission. Once approved, you'll receive your verification badge.

Why should I use Sogolo.com?

Using Sogolo.com ensures safe, transparent transactions with verified sellers. As a seller, verification increases your credibility and sales. As a buyer, you get peace of mind knowing products are authentic and sellers are verified.
