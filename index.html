Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using Sogolo

Sogolo uses an escrow system that holds your payment securely until you confirm receipt of the product. The seller only receives payment after you verify that the item matches the description and meets your expectations. If there's any issue, our team steps in to resolve the dispute fairly.

Product verification typically takes 1-2 business days. Our team carefully inspects photos, product details, and seller information to ensure authenticity. Once verified, you'll receive a notification and can proceed with payment. For high-value items, we may conduct additional checks for your protection.

If the product doesn't match the verified description, you can reject it and receive a full refund. Simply contact our support team within 24 hours of delivery with photos and details. We'll investigate the issue and ensure you're protected. Your satisfaction and safety are our top priorities.

Sogolo charges a small service fee (typically 2-5% of the transaction value) to cover verification, escrow services, and customer support. The exact fee is shown before you confirm payment, so there are no surprises. Both buyers and sellers benefit from the security and peace of mind our platform provides.
