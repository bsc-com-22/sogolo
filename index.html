Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using Sogolo for secure transactions

When you make a purchase, your payment is held securely by Sogolo until the product is verified and delivered. Once you confirm receipt and satisfaction, the funds are released to the seller. This protects both parties in the transaction.

We accept multiple payment methods including Airtel Money, TNM Mpamba, bank transfers, and major credit/debit cards. All payments are processed securely through encrypted channels.

Product verification typically takes 24-48 hours depending on the item and location. Our inspectors work efficiently to ensure quick turnaround while maintaining thorough quality checks.

If our inspector finds that the product doesn't match the seller's description, the transaction can be cancelled and your funds will be returned in full. Your satisfaction and safety are our top priorities.

We charge a small service fee that covers escrow protection, product verification, and secure payment processing. The fee is transparently displayed before you complete any transaction.

Sign up for a seller account, complete your profile with valid identification, and start accepting verification links from buyers. As you complete successful transactions, you'll earn trust scores and verification badges.
