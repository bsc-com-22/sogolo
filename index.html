Everything you need to know about using Sogolo

Sogolo uses secure escrow services to hold your payment until the product is verified and delivered. Your money is never released to the seller until you confirm receipt of the exact item you ordered. This ensures complete protection against fraud and gives you peace of mind with every transaction.
Our verification process typically takes 2-4 hours during business hours. Our expert team carefully inspects product details, images, and seller information to ensure authenticity. For urgent transactions, we offer priority verification that can be completed within 1 hour for a small additional fee.
If the product doesn't match the verified description, you can reject it and receive a full refund. Simply contact our support team within 24 hours of delivery with photos and details. We'll investigate the issue and ensure you're protected. Your satisfaction and safety are our top priorities.
Sogolo charges a small service fee of 3% of the transaction value to cover verification, escrow services, and customer support. This fee is split between buyer and seller (1.5% each). There are no hidden charges, and the fee is clearly displayed before you complete any transaction.
